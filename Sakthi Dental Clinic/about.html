About Sakthi Dental Clinic

Founded by Dr. Anupriya, a clinician with over 20 years of experience, our clinic is committed to gentle, modern dental care.

Our Founder: Dr. Anupriya

With 20+ years of clinical experience, Dr. Anupriya leads a team focused on women’s and pediatric dental health. Her philosophy balances evidence-based dentistry with a compassionate, patient-first approach.

She has helped thousands of families in Hosur with preventive and restorative treatments, ensuring comfort and clarity at every step.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To deliver safe, high-quality, and empathetic dental care to every patient, especially women and children.

Vision: To be Hosur’s most trusted family dental clinic, known for innovation, transparency, and excellent outcomes.
